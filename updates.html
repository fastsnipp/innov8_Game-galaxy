Subway surfurs

Subway Surfers is an endless runner mobile game where players take on the role of a young graffiti artist who is caught in the act by a grumpy inspector and his dog. The game takes place in a vibrant, colorful subway environment, where the player must dodge trains, jump over obstacles, and collect coins while running through a series of train tracks.

Stickman Gun Fire

Stickman Gunfire is a fast-paced action shooter game where players control a stickman character armed with various weapons. The objective is to defeat waves of enemies by shooting, dodging, and using different combat strategies. The game's minimalist stickman art style contrasts with its intense gameplay, featuring dynamic gunfights, explosive action, and a wide variety of weapons to unlock and upgrade.

Tic Tac Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. The objective is to be the first player to form a straight line of three of their own symbols, either "X" or "O," vertically, horizontally, or diagonally. Players take turns marking one empty square on the grid with their symbol, aiming to block their opponent while trying to complete their own line of three. The game is simple, quick to play, and often ends in a tie, or "draw," if neither player can form a line before the grid is filled.
